How to play
  • 🔄 Tap a creature, then a neighbour, to swap them (or swipe one into the other).
  • ✨ Line up 3 or more of a kind — they bloom and clear for score.
  • 🌊 Match 4 to form a current (stripe) that sweeps a whole row or column.
  • 💎 Match 5 to form a pearl — swap it to clear every creature of one colour.
  • 🦟 Match 5 in an L or T shape to form an urchin — it blasts everything around it.
  • 🏁 Reach the target before your moves run out to surface and unlock the next Depth.
Combos — swap two specials together
  • ➕ Current + current — a full cross: whole row and column.
  • 💥 Current + urchin — three rows and three columns.
  • 🌪 Urchin + urchin — one massive blast.
  • 🌟 Pearl + current — every creature of that colour becomes a current and fires.
  • 🌠 Pearl + urchin — every creature of that colour explodes.
  • 💫 Pearl + pearl — clears the entire pool.
Boosters
  • 🦀 Crab Claw (60🐚) — tap the Claw, then tap any creature to pop it free. Costs no move. Clawing a current or pearl sets it off!
  • 🌊 Rip Current (40🐚) — stirs the whole pool into a fresh layout when you can't find a good swap. Costs no move.
  • 💨 Second Wind (100🐚) — when you run out of moves just short of the target, keep the dive going with 5 more moves.
Earning shells 🐚
  • 🏆 Win a Depth: 20–40🐚 (deeper pays more) +10🐚 per star.
  • 🎁 Daily Dive chest: opens on your first visit each day. Rewards climb for 7 straight days (20 → 150🐚), then the cycle restarts. Miss a day and the tide washes you back to day 1.
  • 🎯 Daily Challenge: one featured Depth a day (same for everyone) — reach its 3-star score for +100🐚. Retry as often as you like; your campaign progress is untouched.
  • ⭐ Star milestones: reaching 10, 25, 50, 100… total stars pays +100🐚 each. Replaying old Depths for 3 stars counts!
  • 🔥 Tide's Favor (free): win streaks start your next dive with gift specials — 1 win → a current, 2 → two currents, 3+ → two currents and a pearl. A loss resets it.
